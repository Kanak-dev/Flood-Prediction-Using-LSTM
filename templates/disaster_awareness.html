Emergency Preparedness Kit

Water

One gallon per person per day for at least three days

Food

Non-perishable food for at least three days

First Aid Kit

Including medicines and medical supplies

Flashlight & Batteries

With extra batteries for emergency lighting

Radio

Battery-powered or hand-crank radio for emergency information

Disaster Safety Tips

Know your area's evacuation routes and shelter locations.

Have a family communication plan and meeting point.

Keep important documents in waterproof containers and sign up for emergency alerts.

Learn basic first aid, CPR, and how to shut off utilities. Have emergency contacts ready.
