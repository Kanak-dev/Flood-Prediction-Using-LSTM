Emergency Preparedness Kit
  • Water
    One gallon per person per day for at least three days.
  • Food
    Non-perishable food for at least three days.
  • First Aid Kit
    Medicines and essential medical supplies.
  • Flashlight & Batteries
    Include extra batteries for emergency lighting.
  • Radio
    Battery-powered or hand-crank radio for emergency updates.
Disaster Safety Tips
  • Know your area's evacuation routes and shelter locations
  • Have a family communication plan and meeting point
  • Keep important documents in waterproof containers
  • Sign up for emergency alerts and warnings
  • Learn basic first aid and CPR
  • Locate and learn how to shut off utilities
  • Have emergency contact information readily available
